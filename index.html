Voice capture

Tap the microphone, speak out loud, then review the text before saving. Voice capture uses your browser. Chrome and Edge usually work best. Safari may not support it.

YouTube capture

Paste a YouTube video link. Your app will get the title and thumbnail. Then you will open YouTube's transcript panel, copy the transcript, paste it here, and save it.

After loading, use the transcript button below. If YouTube does not open the transcript automatically, click the video description or three-dot menu and choose “Show transcript.”

PDF capture

Drop a PDF file here, or tap the box to choose a file. The text is extracted inside your browser, which means the PDF does not need to be sent to a new third-party service.

URL capture

Paste the webpage link and then paste the article text below. Browsers block most websites from being automatically read by another website. This is a security rule that protects you, so manual paste is the reliable self-sovereign method here.
